# Game Design Document for Chrono Squad

## 1. Game Overview
Chrono Squad is a cooperative multiplayer action-adventure game set in a stylized time-warped universe. Players will embark on missions across various timelines to combat historical enigmas and defend the fabric of reality. 

## 2. Core Systems
- **Player Characters**: Players can choose from a roster of unique heroes, each with distinct abilities linked to different timelines.
- **Time Manipulation Mechanic**: Players can slow down or rewind time to solve puzzles and defeat enemies.
- **Level Progression**: Players will earn experience points and unlock new abilities as they progress through the game.

## 3. Mechanics
- **Combat System**: Real-time combat that blends melee and ranged attacks, along with special time abilities.
- **Puzzle Solving**: Players will face puzzles that require time manipulation to progress.
- **Co-op Features**: Players can combine their abilities for powerful combo attacks and time effects.

## 4. Art/Audio/Tech Stack  
- **Art Style**: A colorful, cel-shaded style with an emphasis on exaggerated character designs.
- **Audio**: A rich, adaptive soundtrack that reacts to gameplay and time fluctuations.
- **Tech Stack**: Built on Unity with a focus on optimizing network performance for seamless multiplayer experiences.

## 5. MVP Scoping
The Minimum Viable Product will include:
- Three playable characters.
- Two distinct levels, each featuring unique enemies and challenges.
- Basic time manipulation mechanics.
- A simple tutorial to introduce players to core mechanics.

## 6. Example Progression Flow
Players start at the base timeline, completing missions that introduce them to time manipulation. 
1. **Mission 1**: Tutorial level featuring combat basics.
2. **Mission 2**: Introduces puzzles using time manipulation. 
3. **Mission 3**: A challenging co-op level requiring teamwork to succeed.

## 7. Monetization
The game will use a one-time purchase model with potential DLC for new characters and timelines.

## 8. Marketing Strategy
- Utilize social media platforms to build a community.
- Engage with gaming influencers for gameplay showcases.
- Launch a beta testing phase to gather player feedback.

## 9. Future Updates
- Introduce new timelines and characters based on player feedback.
- Enhance the story with seasonal events and challenges.

## 10. Conclusion
Chrono Squad aims to provide an engaging multiplayer experience that combines fast-paced action with innovative time manipulation mechanics. With a focus on collaboration and strategic gameplay, it seeks to capture the imagination of players.
