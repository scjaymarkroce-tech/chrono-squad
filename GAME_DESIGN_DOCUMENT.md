# Game Design Document

## Game Overview
### Title: Chrono Squad
Chrono Squad is a futuristic action-adventure game that takes players on a time-bending journey through different eras and dimensions. Players will assume the role of elite time agents tasked with preventing a cataclysmic event that threatens the space-time continuum.

## Goals
- **Engaging Storyline:** Develop an immersive narrative that captivates players and compels them to progress through the game.
- **Dynamic Gameplay:** Create a mix of combat, exploration, and puzzle-solving that utilizes the unique mechanics of time manipulation.
- **Artistic Cohesion:** Ensure a visually stunning experience that marries the aesthetic of each era within the game.

## Core Systems
- **Time Manipulation Engine:** A core system that allows players to manipulate time, including slowing down, speeding up, and rewinding events.
- **Combat System:** An intuitive combat system that incorporates time-based moves and abilities.
- **Inventory Management:** A robust system for managing gear, upgrades, and collectibles through the journey.

## Mechanics
- **Time Slow:** Players can slow down time to navigate through complex enemy patterns or environmental hazards.
- **Time Rewind:** Players can rewind time momentarily to avoid damage or correct mistakes.
- **Time Travel:** Players can travel to different historical periods, each with unique challenges and enemies.

## Features
- **Multiple Eras:** Explore various timelines, including the Dinosaur Age, Medieval Times, and a Dystopian Future.
- **Upgradable Skills:** Players can enhance their time manipulation abilities and combat skills through a skill tree.
- **Co-op Multiplayer:** Team up with friends to tackle challenges and share time-manipulation mechanics.

## Art/Audio/Technical Details
- **Visual Style:** A mix of stylized realism for environments, with vibrant colors reflecting different time periods.
- **Sound Design:** An adaptive audio system that changes the soundtrack based on gameplay elements and player actions.
- **Technical Performance:** Optimized for both PC and console platforms, aiming for 60 FPS at maximum graphical settings.

## Example Progression Flow
1. **Tutorial:** Players begin in a controlled environment to learn the basics of time manipulation mechanics.
2. **First Mission:** Travel to the Medieval Times to prevent an artifact theft that could disrupt the timeline.
3. **Skill Acquisition:** Complete challenges to unlock new skills and improve time manipulation.
4. **Boss Encounter:** Face a time-warped knight that utilizes time manipulation against the player.
5. **Eras Unlocking:** Complete missions to unlock new eras and deepen the narrative complexity as new challenges arise.
