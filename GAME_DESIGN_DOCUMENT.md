# Game Design Document

## Title
Roguelike Character Collection Turn-Based Battler

## Overview
This game is a turn-based battler featuring roguelike elements with a focus on character collection and gacha mechanics. Players will progress through dungeons, facing challenges and collecting characters without any time manipulation, eras, or multiplayer features.

## Key Features
- **Character Collection:** Collect various unique characters, each with distinct abilities and designs.
- **Turn-Based Combat:** Engage in strategic turn-based combat utilizing the strengths of your collected characters.
- **Dungeon Progression:** Explore and progress through increasingly challenging dungeons.
- **Gacha Mechanics:** Use in-game currency to acquire new characters through a gacha system.

## Exclusions
- No time manipulation mechanics
- No eras in gameplay
- No multiplayer features
