# ENEMIES

## 1. Headless Swordsman (NO NAME YET)
- **Element:** METAL
- **Class:** ELITE
- **Stats:** High health, High defense, High attack, Slow speed
- **Passive:** Immune to stun
- **Skills:**
  - Normal: (Single Target) Slash the heroes with sword, deals metal damage

## 2. Mage (NO NAME YET)
- **Element:** WATER, FIRE, LIGHTNING, NATURE (depends on their wand)
- **Class:** COMMON
- **Stats:** Low health, High attack, Low defense, Slow speed
- **Passive:** Has a shield that grants them 30% damage reduction
- **Skills:**
  - Normal: (Single Target) Cast a ball to the characters (ex. Fire ball)

## 3. Cursed Painter (NO NAME YET)
- **Element:** NATURE
- **Class:** COMMON
- **Stats:** Low health, Average attack, Average defense, Average speed
- **Passive:** Resurrects after dying (once per fight, at 50% HP)
- **Skills:**
  - Normal: Painter's Wish (Mass Attack) Splash an ink to the character dealing nature damage

## 4. Shen
- **Element:** LIGHTNING
- **Class:** COMMON
- **Stats:** Low health, Low attack, Average defense, Extremely fast
- **Passive:** Every 3rd round, attack deals stun to character
- **Skills:**
  - Normal: Electricks (Mass Attack) shock the character

## 5. Dragon Head (NO NAME YET)
- **Element:** FIRE
- **Class:** ELITE
- **Stats:** High health, High damage, High defense, Average speed
- **Passive:** Summons a mage every round (round start + every 4th round)
- **Skills:**
  - Normal: Dragon's Breath – (Mass Attack) fires from its mouth dealing fire damage

## 6. Nautica
- **Element:** WATER
- **Class:** COMMON
- **Stats:** Average health, Low attack, Very high defense, Low speed
- **Passive:** Starts round with 10% damage reduction for the whole enemy team, increases 5% per round up to 35%
- **Skills:**
  - Normal: Shield Spam - (Mass Buff) grants 10% damage reduction

## 7. Grim Reaper (NO NAME YET)
- **Element:** VOID
- **Class:** BOSS
- **Stats:** Extremely high health, Extremely high attack, Average speed
- **Passive:** Always spawn Nautica on its side
- **Skills:**
  - Normal: Slash - (Mass Attack) Swings weapon and deals damage
  - Ultimate: Final Breath – (Single Target) has a sure kill to an unshielded character, if a shielded character is attacked, Grim damage decreased to 30% (Cooldown: Every 5th round)
